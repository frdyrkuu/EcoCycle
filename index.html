Our Mission

At Eco-Cyle, our mission is to create a sustainable future by promoting eco-friendly practices and reducing waste through user incentives. We believe that by encouraging responsible waste disposal, we can make a significant impact on our environment and preserve it for future generations.
Our waste management system uses RFID technology to track and sort waste, making it easier and more convenient for users to dispose of their waste appropriately. By rewarding users with points for responsible waste disposal, we hope to incentivize eco-friendly practices and encourage more people to adopt sustainable habits.
